## Privacy Policy

### What data is collected

Super XO does not require an account and does not ask for your name,
email, or any personal identifying information to play.

- **Locally, on your device**: your theme, language, sound, and
  display preferences, plus the code of the last online room you
  played in (so the app can offer to reconnect you if you get
  disconnected). This data stays on your device and is never sent
  anywhere except back to the game server when relevant to gameplay
  (e.g. rejoining a room).
- **On the game server, only while you're playing online**: a
  randomly generated, anonymous player ID (not tied to your identity
  in any way), the moves you make, and your connection's IP address,
  the same basic connection information any server sees from any
  incoming connection. This information is held in the server's
  memory only for the duration of your session and is not written to
  any database or permanent storage. It is cleared when the server
  restarts or your game session ends.

### Tracking and third parties

Super XO does not use third-party ad networks, analytics SDKs, or any
tracking technology. No data about you is shared with third parties
for advertising or tracking purposes, and none of your activity is
tracked across other apps or websites.

### How your data is used

Solely to make the game work: matching you with an opponent, syncing
moves between players, and letting you reconnect if your connection
drops mid-game. Nothing is used for any other purpose.

### Children's privacy

Super XO is not directed at children under 13, and the developer does
not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

### Your rights

Since no persistent account or personal data is stored, there is
generally nothing to access, export, or delete beyond what already
lives locally on your own device (which you can clear at any time by
uninstalling the app or clearing its local storage). If you have any
question about data handling, contact Sodium11work@gmail.com.

### Security

Connections to the game server use standard encrypted transport
(WSS/HTTPS). As with any online service, no method of transmission is
100% secure, but no sensitive personal data is transmitted in the
first place.

### Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the app changes. Continued use of the
app after an update means you accept the revised policy. The
effective date above will always reflect the latest version.
